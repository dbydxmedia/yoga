Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be flexible to start?

Absolutely not! This class is designed for complete beginners. We'll start with gentle movements to build flexibility and strength over time.

What equipment do I need?

A stable internet connection is all you really need. A yoga mat is optional but recommended for comfort.

What platform are the classes on?

Classes are held online via a video conferencing platform like Zoom or Google Meet. The link will be shared with you after your booking is confirmed.

What if I'm late or miss a class?

Please try to join on time for the best experience. The fee covers access to the live sessions, and recordings are not provided.

Getting Started Checklist

Before your first class, make sure you have:

  • A quiet space with a stable internet connection.
  • A yoga mat or a non-slip surface.
  • The class link (sent to your email after booking confirmation).
  • A good camera placement so the instructor can help with your alignment.
